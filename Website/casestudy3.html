Case Study 3

Boston Dynamics: Atlas

Atlas is just one of the many impressive robots created by Boston Dynamics, and is a bipedal walking robot capable of many tasks.

Built from a lightweight 3D printed chassis, Atlas uses on-board custom battery and hydraulic power unit that uses its 28 hydraulic joints to move around almost human like.

Atlas also contains advanced depth sensors to detect it's environment which runs through it's control system, utilising algorithms involving its whole body and surroundings to plan movements.
