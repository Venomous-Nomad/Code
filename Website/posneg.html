Positives and Negatives

Advantages and Disadvantages

While robotics has massive benefits to society and work as a whole, such as:

  • Cost effectiveness (robots don't need lunch breaks or holidays to work)
  • Increased productivity (repetitive tasks can be done with ease)
  • Work quality assurance (tasks can be done accurately to the nearest millimeter)

There are also major downsides to robotics, such as:

  • Automation replacing human jobs (people losing jobs to automation which is a major concern)
  • Cost of advanced robotic systems being high
  • Skilled individuals with knowledge required for programming and managing systems needed for use
