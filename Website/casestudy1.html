Case Study 1

Mars Rovers

Created by NASA's scientists and engineers, multiple 'rovers' have been deployed to Mars for exploration and scientific discovery.

The Mars rover such as the Perserverance rover is a car sized robot that scans the surface of Mars for signs of biosignatures in rock samples.

Just some tools that the Mars rover Curiosity is equiped with include:

  • Radiation detector
  • Mastcam camera for high quality images
  • ChemCam that can identify elemental composition of material

Because of these robots, we have discovered that Mars had the suitable chemistry for living microbes. What was also found is sulfur, oxygen, nitrogen, phosphorus and carbon. These elements are needed for life to occur, and gives us hope for the planet Mars as another home.
